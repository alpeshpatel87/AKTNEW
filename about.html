About Our Company

Safe Logistic & Transport Solutions That Saves Our Valuable Time!

AKT Logistics is a part of Inter India Group (a 25 year old Group) which is led by two young entrepreneur Mr. Vikas Tanwar and Mr. Ankit Tanwar. Changing the method from the former tradition to the method of the younger generation, AKT Logistics work with state-of-the-art equipment that enables transport according to the highest quality guidelines We are driven to provide the most responsive, customer-focused and flexible logistics solutions to exceed our client's expectations.

INTER INDIA GROUP: The Inter India Group, a privately-owned ISO 2008:9001 certified and Approved by Indian Banks Association Total Logistics company engaged in diversified activities, from its strategic location Gandhidham-Gujarat, with a group worth of approx Rs 300 Crores, having presence over 29 cities in India.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make AKT Logistics the strongest global logistics and supply chain management company as a trusted partner for all our clients. We are committed to providing our customers multi-mode services according to their needs.

Our Mission

Our Mission is “To manage and coordinate the flow of the products from the supplier point to the consumer's hand.” Because we believe the reputation of a service provider depends on the quality of their services.
